Welcome to my website! I am a Research Associate at the [Departamento de Fundamentos del Análisis Económico (FAE)](https://dfae.ua.es/en/home.html), [University of Alicante](https://www.ua.es), working on the EU Next Generation-funded project *Macroeconomic Risks and Financial Stability*, in collaboration with the [International Housing Observatory](https://int.housing-observatory.com/). My research interests are in the fields of macroeconomics, housing markets, and financial stability.
